Pancake

Description

Fluffy, soft pancakes made from scratch with basic ingredients. Perfect for breakfast or brunch, served with your favorite toppings like syrup, butter, or fresh fruits.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups (190g) all-purpose flour
  • 3 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 ¼ cups (300ml) milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons (45g) melted butter

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Pour the wet mixture into the dry ingredients.
  4. Stir until just combined (batter may be a little lumpy).
  5. Heat a lightly oiled pan over medium heat.
  6. Pour about ¼ cup of batter for each pancake.
  7. Cook until bubbles form and edges look set.
  8. Flip and cook until golden brown on both sides.
  9. Repeat with remaining batter.
  10. Serve with syrup, butter, or fruits.
