Cheese-cake

Desciption

A classic, creamy cheesecake with a buttery graham cracker crust and a rich, smooth filling. Perfect for any occasion, it’s easy to make and always a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

For the crust

  • 1 ½ cups (150g) graham cracker crumbs (or digestive biscuits)
  • ¼ cup (50g) sugar
  • ½ cup (115g) unsalted butter, melted

For the Filling

  • ⅔ cup (160ml) heavy cream
  • ⅔ cup (160ml) sour cream
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup (200g) sugar
  • 4 packages (8 oz each, 900g total) cream cheese, softened

Optional topping

  • Berries and fruits
  • white/milk chocolate

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (160°C).
  2. Mix graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter.
  3. Press the mixture into the bottom of a springform pan.
  4. Bake the crust for 10 minutes and let it cool.
  5. Beat the softened cream cheese until smooth.
  6. Gradually add sugar and vanilla extract, mixing well.
  7. Add eggs one at a time, mixing after each addition.
  8. Stir in sour cream and heavy cream until smooth.
  9. Pour the filling into the cooled crust.
  10. Bake for 55-70 minutes until the center is almost set.
  11. Turn off the oven, crack the door slightly, and let it cool inside for 1 hour.
  12. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  13. Top with berries or fruit sauce and serve.
